# Nutritional Partner

Nutritional Partner is an intelligent, agent-driven assistant designed to transform the way health-conscious individuals and athletes manage their clinical nutritional plans. Specifically tailored for **those who exercise and work with professional nutritionists**, the application bridges the gap between static dietary prescriptions and the dynamic needs of a fitness-oriented lifestyle.

## Core Features

### 📄 Intelligent Plan Ingestion
Upload your professional nutritional plan in PDF format. The assistant automatically identifies and extracts:
- **Daily Nutritional Requirements**: Macros, calories, and specific dietary goals.
- **Dietary Constraints**: Allergies, medical restrictions, and food intolerances.
- **Personal Preferences**: Favorite cuisines, food likes, and dislikes.

### 🧠 Profile Completion Agent
If your plan is missing specific details needed for a comprehensive profile, an intelligent agent engages with you through an interactive interface to fill the gaps, ensuring your nutritional profile is always accurate and actionable.

### 🔄 Dynamic Food Equivalents
Never worry about missing a specific ingredient again. Ask the assistant questions like *"How can I replace 120g of steak?"* and get immediate, scientifically-backed alternatives. The system queries standard nutritional equivalent systems (like SMAE) to provide options that maintain your dietary balance.

### 📊 Agentic Dashboard 
Interact with your data through more than just text. The application features a generative, interactive UI where the assistant updates visual charts, meal tracking widgets, and dietary checklists in real-time as your conversation progresses.

## Goal
The mission of Nutritional Partner is to provide high-fidelity dietary adherence by making nutritional data interactive, flexible, and always relevant to your immediate needs.
